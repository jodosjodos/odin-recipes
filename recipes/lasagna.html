Lasagna Recipe

Description

Delicious homemade lasagna with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  2. In a skillet, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic until the beef is browned.
  3. Add tomato sauce, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, and salt.
  5. Spread a layer of meat sauce in a baking dish. Place a layer of cooked noodles on top.
  6. Add a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture and sprinkle with mozzarella cheese.
  7. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of mozzarella cheese on top.
  8. Bake in the oven at 375°F (190°C) for 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Let it cool for a few minutes, then serve and enjoy!

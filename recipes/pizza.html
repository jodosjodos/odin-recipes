Pizza Recipe

Description

Delicious homemade pizza with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Assorted toppings (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives)
  • Olive oil
  • Dried oregano
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to the recommended temperature for the pizza dough.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  4. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  6. Add your favorite toppings to the pizza.
  7. Drizzle olive oil over the toppings and sprinkle with dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for the specified time, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve!
